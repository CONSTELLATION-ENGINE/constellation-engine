What are these 4 roles?
Main User chat — every message hits this. Pick the smartest model. Consolidation Memory hygiene — distill, dream, FUSE/SUPERSEDE. Wants reasoning depth. Heads-up: Enabling Telegram routes your bot token, conversation messages, and any media you send through Telegram Messenger LLC's servers. Telegram is a third-party service we do not control. Their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy apply to all data you send. Skip this stage to keep everything local.
